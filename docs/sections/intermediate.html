Recommended Prerequisites

What You'll Learn

ML Fundamentals

Understand supervised vs unsupervised learning, the train/test split, and why overfitting is the enemy of good models.

Model Evaluation

Go beyond accuracy. Master precision, recall, F1, ROC-AUC, and know which metric matters for your specific problem.

Neural Networks

From perceptrons to backpropagation. Understand how deep networks learn representations layer by layer.

LLMs & Transformers

Demystify attention mechanisms, how transformers work, and why they revolutionized natural language processing.

Embeddings & Semantic Search

Learn how text and images become vectors, and use similarity search to build intelligent search systems.

RAG Pipelines

Retrieval-Augmented Generation: ground LLMs in real documents to eliminate hallucinations and add knowledge cutoff independence.

AI App Development

Put it all together. Build full-stack AI applications with streaming, tool use, conversation memory, and API integrations.
